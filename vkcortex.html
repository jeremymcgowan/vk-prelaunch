AI Engineering FAQ

Is our data used to train public AI models?
Absolutely not. When we deploy VK Cortex, we utilize private enterprise endpoints. Your data, prompts, and outputs are strictly walled off and are never utilized by OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google to train their foundational public models. Your intellectual property remains securely yours.
What is RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)?
RAG is the gold standard for enterprise AI. Instead of asking an AI to "guess" an answer based on its general training, RAG architecture searches your private company documents first, retrieves the exact relevant paragraphs, and forces the AI to construct its answer based purely on those internal facts. It practically eliminates "hallucinations" and ensures absolute accuracy.
Do we need our own software engineers to use this?
No. V&K Partners acts as your dedicated AI engineering team. We architect the infrastructure, build the integrations into your existing CRM or databases, and deliver a polished, user-friendly interface that your non-technical staff can use immediately.
