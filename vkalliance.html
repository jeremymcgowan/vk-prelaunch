VK Alliance & Equity FAQ

How do I qualify for the Founding 100 Equity Grant?
The Founding 100 grant is reserved for the first one hundred enterprises that successfully deploy and maintain the core V&K infrastructure modules (Launch, Flow, Shield, People, and Cortex). Once your migration is complete and your account is active, your firm is automatically issued 1,000 shares of private equity in Vanderbilt & Knight Partners LLC, subject to standard vesting and operating agreement terms.
Is the executive network truly private?
Yes. The VK Alliance network is strictly confidential. We enforce rigorous non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) among all members to ensure that strategies, deal flow, and operational metrics can be discussed openly and securely without risk of public exposure.
Can V&K assist in preparing our company for acquisition?
Absolutely. Preparing an enterprise for a lucrative exit requires flawless technical, financial, and legal due diligence. Our architects work alongside your team to ensure your infrastructure, cap table, and revenue pipelines are structurally sound and highly attractive to institutional buyers.
